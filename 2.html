INTEREST IN IT




 

 


    I can't forget my first smartphone when I was 11 years old. I was so excited to get a new toy that I used to change all kinds of UI settings, starting with "Rooting." However, my smartphone eventually broke down and I often went to and from repair centers. But I didn't give up and did many experiments with my smartphone. Even before college entrance. Maybe that's why I got a bitter taste in the college entrance exam.

    Korean high school curriculum is divided into liberal arts and natural sciences. I was basically not interested in science like physics, so I belonged to liberal arts. Students in liberal arts cannot apply to science colleges like engineering college, pharmacy, or medical school.

  Rather, I thought it was a good thing and looked for foreign universities where I could enter engineering colleges regardless of liberal arts or natural sciences. Among them, RMIT stood out. Worried about living alone in another country, my parents recommended Vietnam among Australia and Vietnam, and promised to go to Australia as an exchange student later.

 


 


  Now, I'm taking online classes in Australia as Corona makes it impossible to leave the country unexpectedly. It is a little hard to take a class in English after studying only in my native Korean all my life, but I will continue to work hard and study. I would like to acquire basic knowledge of IT here, and furthermore, I would like to learn more about programming that I am interested in among the numerous fields of IT.
